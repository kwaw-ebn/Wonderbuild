Interlocking Bricks vs Cement Blocks: Which is Better in Ghana?

Choosing the right building material is one of the most important decisions when constructing a house in Ghana. Two popular options are interlocking bricks and traditional cement blocks. But which one is better?

1. Construction Speed

Interlocking bricks are designed to fit together without mortar, making construction significantly faster. Cement blocks require cement bonding, which slows down the process.

Winner: Interlocking Bricks

2. Cost Efficiency

Interlocking bricks reduce the need for cement and lower labor costs, making them more affordable overall. Cement blocks require more materials and labor.

Winner: Interlocking Bricks

3. Strength & Durability

Both materials are durable, but interlocking bricks provide better alignment and structural consistency.

4. Sustainability

Interlocking bricks use less cement, making them more environmentally friendly and sustainable.

Final Verdict

Interlocking bricks outperform cement blocks in speed, cost, and efficiency. For modern construction in Ghana, they are becoming the preferred choice.
